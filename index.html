About Us

JM Charo is a peanut brokerage firm that helps customers source peanuts and peanut products from leading peanut shellers. We work with suppliers in the USA, Argentina, and Brazil; and we work with manufacturers across the globe in North America, Central/South America, Europe, and Asia.

We are very active in the world peanut markets, gaining insights and seeing trends in real time. We share insights not only from active participation in the peanut market, but also from our best-in-class peanut market data platform "PnutX". We take pride in helping our customers to be strategic in their procurement decisions.
